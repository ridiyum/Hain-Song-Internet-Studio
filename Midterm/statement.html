Design Statement

Visual & Interaction Design Choices

For this Bahamas destination website, I chose a color palette inspired by the Bahamian flag and natural environment. The primary colors are aqua blue (#00A0B0), golden yellow (#FFC72C), and black (#000000), which represent the ocean, sun, and strength of the Bahamian people. These colors immediately evoke the tropical feeling of the islands while maintaining good readability and visual hierarchy.

The typography uses two complementary Google Fonts: 'Open Sans' for body text due to its excellent readability on screens, and 'Montserrat' for headings to create visual interest and hierarchy. The navigation menu uses a clean, centered layout with rounded buttons that provide clear visual feedback when hovered. CTA buttons are prominently placed on the homepage to encourage user engagement, using the contrasting yellow color to draw attention while maintaining the overall color scheme.

Image selection focuses on high-quality, vibrant photos that showcase the beauty of the Bahamas - crystal clear waters, white sandy beaches, and cultural experiences. The layout uses ample white space to prevent visual clutter and let the images speak for themselves. Each activity and restaurant section follows a consistent pattern of image followed by descriptive text, creating a rhythm that makes the content easy to scan and digest. The overall design aims to be inviting, easy to navigate, and reflective of the relaxing yet exciting vacation experience the Bahamas offers.
