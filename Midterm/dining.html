Delicious Dining in the Bahamas

Ocean Breeze Grill

Enjoy fresh seafood with spectacular ocean views. Our signature dish is the grilled lobster with garlic butter, served with traditional peas and rice. The perfect spot for a romantic dinner as you watch the sunset.

Conch Shell Café

Experience authentic Bahamian cuisine at its best. Try our famous cracked conch, conch fritters, and conch salad. Located in the heart of Nassau, this family-owned restaurant has been serving locals and tourists for over 30 years.

Palm Beach Bar & Grill

Casual dining right on the sand. Enjoy burgers, sandwiches, and tropical drinks with your toes in the water. Live music on weekends and the best piña coladas on the island make this a must-visit spot.
