Activities I Want to Experience in the Bahamas

Snorkeling & Diving

I would love to discover the incredible underwater world of the Bahamas. Swimming with tropical fish, exploring coral reefs, and even encountering friendly dolphins sounds amazing. The clear waters provide perfect visibility for what would be an unforgettable aquatic adventure.

Swim with Pigs

I'm really excited about visiting the famous swimming pigs of Exuma! These friendly pigs live on Big Major Cay and love swimming in the crystal-clear waters. It's a unique experience I won't find anywhere else in the world and something I've always wanted to see.

Island Hopping Tours

Taking a boat tour to explore multiple islands in one day sounds like the perfect way to experience the Bahamas. I'd love to visit secluded beaches, enjoy fresh seafood lunches, and experience the diverse landscapes that make each Bahamian island unique.
