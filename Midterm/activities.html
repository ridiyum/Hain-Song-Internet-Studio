Amazing Activities in the Bahamas

Snorkeling & Diving

Discover the incredible underwater world of the Bahamas. Swim with tropical fish, explore coral reefs, and even encounter friendly dolphins. The clear waters provide perfect visibility for an unforgettable aquatic adventure.

Swim with Pigs

Visit the famous swimming pigs of Exuma! These friendly pigs live on Big Major Cay and love swimming in the crystal-clear waters. It's a unique experience you won't find anywhere else in the world.

Island Hopping Tours

Take a boat tour and explore multiple islands in one day. Visit secluded beaches, enjoy fresh seafood lunches, and experience the diverse landscapes that make each Bahamian island unique.
