Creative brief


Project Name Study Haven Launch Project
Project Timeline 09/16/2025 - 12/03/2025
Project Overview
What problem are we solving? How?
Students in Dallas waste valuable study time searching for appropriate study locations. Study Haven solves this by creating a centralized database of student-focused study spaces with detailed filtering options.

Why are we solving this problem? What's the benefit?
This improves student productivity and academic success by eliminating the frustration of location hunting. Students can focus their energy on actual studying rather than logistics.

How does this problem align with our product/service?
Study Haven directly addresses the gap in location discovery tools by providing student specific criteria like Wi-Fi quality, noise levels, and real-time crowding updates that generic apps don't offer.
Audience
Primary: College students in Dallas (ages 18-25) who study outside their dorms, including commuter students, graduate students, and those seeking collaborative or quiet study environments.

Secondary: High school students preparing for college exams, working professionals pursuing certifications or continuing education, and remote workers seeking productive spaces outside the home.
Competition
University Study Finders (campus-specific tools like UMN's Study Space Finder, limited to single institutions), Google Maps/Yelp (general location data, no study-specific filtering), Study Blog Lists (static articles about "Best Study Spots," outdated information), Virtual Study Spaces (Virtual Cafe and aesthetic study websites, solve ambiance but not physical space discovery).
Key Messages
Pain Point: You waste 30+ minutes every study session just finding a decent spot with the right noise level, Wi-Fi, and available seating.

Experience Without Pain: Imagine knowing exactly where to go based on your specific study needs - whether you need complete silence or group collaboration space - before you even leave home.

Benefit: You arrive ready to focus, with 2+ extra hours of productive study time each week and improved academic performance.

"So What?": Dallas students can reclaim hours of lost study time and actually improve their grades instead of wandering around campus looking for the perfect spot.
Call to Action
Primary: "Find My Study Spot" - Leads users directly to the search/filter interface

Secondary: "Add a Location," "Leave a Review," "Update Status," "Save Favorites"
How to Measure Success
Primary Success Metrics:
- Strong user adoption within first semester
- High conversion rate from searches to location visits
- Comprehensive database of verified Dallas study locations

Quality Indicators:
- Excellent user ratings for location accuracy
- Strong user retention and repeat visits
- Active community engagement through reviews and updates

Project Impact:
- Significant reduction in time spent finding study spaces
- Improved study productivity reported by users
- Active participation from local venues and libraries

Timeline Milestones:
- Early launch with core location database
- Steady user growth and location expansion
- Sustainable platform with strong community engagement
