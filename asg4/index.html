About the Band

Nickelback is a Canadian rock band formed in 1995 in Alberta. The band consists of Chad Kroeger who is the lead vocal and plays guitar, Ryan Peake who is backing vocals but also plays guitar, Mike Kroeger whom takes the role of playing bass, and Daniel Adair who is the drummer of the band.
